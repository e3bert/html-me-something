The Road to Becoming 2015 World Series Champions

  • American League Division Series
  • American League Championship Series
  • World Series
  • World Champs!

    • American League Division Series

    The 2015 Postseason Run started out with a thrilling 5 game series win against the Houston Astros.

    American League Championship Series

    The 2015 Postseason Run continued with a 6 game series win against the Toronto Blue Jays. This lead to the Royals 4th ever appearance in the World Series.

    World Series

    The 2015 Postseason Run concluded with a dominating 5 game series win against the National League Champion New York Mets.

    World Champs!

    The 2015 Postseason Run ended up as the 2nd time the World Series Championship was won by the Kansas City Royals.
